Good Food Can Improve Your Body

Good food provides essential nutrients, supports muscle growth, boosts energy, aids recovery, enhances
metabolism, strengthens immunity, and promotes overall health.

Milk

Milk provides calcium, protein, vitamins D and B12, and riboflavin.

Banana

Bananas offer potassium, vitamin C, vitamin B6, fiber, and carbohydrates.

Egg

Eggs provide protein, vitamin B12, vitamin D, choline, and healthy fats.

Chicken

Chicken offers protein, B vitamins, iron, zinc, and healthy fats.

BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is calculated as weight in kilograms divided by
height in meters squared. It helps assess body weight health.

BMI Calculator Chart
BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
